Alessandro
Buzzi

PhD Candidate  ·  MIT

I design, fabricate, and control integrated photonic systems for computing and quantum technologies.

Research

Quantum photonics & color centers

MEMS-based spectral tuning and nanoscale localization of silicon color centers. Foundry-compatible integration of diamond quantum microchiplets for scalable quantum photonic systems.

Heterogeneous photonic integration

Micro-transfer printing of lasers, gain media, nonlinear materials, and detectors onto photonic platforms for heterogeneous integration of active devices.

AI-driven characterization & control

Machine learning and automation workflows for photonic crystal cavity analysis, process-parameter extraction, and large-scale optimization of optical interconnects and photonic computing devices.
